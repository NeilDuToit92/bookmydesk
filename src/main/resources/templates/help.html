Booking a desk

  • 1. Select the date you want to book for
  •   - The '+' and '-' buttons on the keyboard can be used to quickly navigate between dates
  • 2. Click on the desk you want to book
  •   - Green: The desk is available
  •   - Red: The desk has been booked by someone else
  •   - Pink: The desk has been booked by you
  •   - Purple: The desk has been permanently reserved for someone else
  •   - Orange: The desk has been permanently reserved for you
  • 3. Click 'Confirm' to confirm the booking
  • 4. A message will display indicating a successful booking and the desk will turn purple
  • 5. The date navigation bar will indicate green for the days you have a booking for
